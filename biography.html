Biography


Early Life

  • Downey was born in Manhattan, New York City, the youngest of two children. His father, Robert Downey Sr., was an actor and filmmaker, while his mother, Elsie Ann(n√©e Ford), was an actress who appeared in Downey Sr.'s films.Robert's original family name was Elias which was changed by his father to enlist in the Army. Downey and his older sister Allyson grew up in Greenwich Village.As a child, Downey was "surrounded by drugs." His father, a drug addict, allowed Downey to use marijuana at age six, an incident which his father later said he regretted.Downey later stated that drug use became an emotional bond between him and his father: "When my dad and I would do drugs together, it was like him trying to express his love for me in the only way he knew how." Eventually, Downey began spending every night abusing alcohol and "making a thousand phone calls in pursuit of drugs".


    • Personal Life

  • Downey started dating actress Sarah Jessica Parker in 1984 after meeting her on the set of First born. The couple later separated in 1991 due to his drug addiction. Downey married actress and singer Deborah Falconer on May 29, 1992, after a 42-day courtship. Their son, Indio Falconer Downey, was born in September 1993.


    • Career

  • Robert Downey Jr. is an American actor and producer known for his extensive and diverse career in the entertainment industry. He began his acting career at a young age and gained recognition for his role in the film "Less Than Zero"(1987), which showcased his talent even in his early twenties. Downey faced personal and legal troubles in the late 1990s and early 2000s, which led to a hiatus from acting. However, he made a remarkable comeback in the mid-2000s.


  • One of the most significant phases of Downey's career was his portrayal of Tony Stark /Iron Man in Marvel Cinematic Universe(MCU). He first appeared as Iron Man in the 2008 film "Iron Man" and went on to reprise the role in numerous MCU films, becoming an iconic character.


    • Other Ventures

  • Music:Downey has sung on several soundtracks for his films, including for Chaplin, Too Much Sun, Two Girls and a Guy, Friends and Lovers, The Singing Detective, and Kiss Kiss Bang Bang. In 2001, he appeared in the music video for Elton John's song, "I Want Love".

  • Production Company:On June 14, 2010, Downey and his wife Susan opened their own production company called Team Downey. Their first project was The Judge.
  • Religious Belief:Downey has described his religious beliefs as "Jewish Buddhist",[147] and he is reported to have consulted astrologers.[148] In the past, Downey has been interested in Christianity and the Hare Krishna movement.
  • Sobriety: Downey says he has been drug-free since July 2003 and credited his wife with helping him overcome his drug and alcohol habits, along with his family, therapy, meditation, twelve-step recovery programs, yoga, and the practice of Wing Chun kung fu,the martial art he learned from Eric Oram, who is also a fight consultant in several of Downey's movies.
